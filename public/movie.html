The Best Movie Acording to Radu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Once Upon a Time in America

The best gangster movie. It presents from an epic perspective major themes of friendship, memories, violence and betrayal.

Scent of a Woman

Reservist Colonel Frank Slade, now blind, enlists the help of a shy college boy.

Star Wars

The best SF movie. The events described in Star Wars take place in a fictional galaxy.
